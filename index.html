Acknowledgments¶

The python files that served as a starting point for the development of this library were originally developed by the St. Lawrence Global Observatory (OGSL) for a specific project funded by the Department of Fisheries and Oceans Canada.

Elements of the development was support by NOAA’s Integreated Ocean Observing System, and the Ocean Tracking Network.
